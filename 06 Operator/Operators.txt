OPERATORS

1. Arithmatic Operator
   +,-,*,\,%,++,--
2. Relational Operator
   ==,!=,>,<,>=,<=
3. Logical Operator
   &&, ||, !
4. Bitwise Operator
   &,|,^,~,<<,>>
5. Assignment Operator
   =,+=,-=,*=,/=,%=,<<=,>>=,&=,^=
6. Misc Operator
   sizeof(), &(Return Address), *(Pointer to Variable), ?:(Conditional Expression)

   ()Function Call, []Array Element, ->(indirect member selection), .(direct member selection)
